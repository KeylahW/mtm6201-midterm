Why did I choose photography?

I chose photography for my website because I enjoy taking pictures of different things. My favorite subjects to photograph are my dogs and plants.
I enjoy taking photos of plants because they are easily found everywhere and are interesting to look at. I also enjoy taking photos of my dogs because I think that they
would look nice in photos.

There are many different locations that I enjoy taking photos at.
One location that I enjoy to take photos at is my cottage. I like taking pictures there because it is surrounded
by a forest and gives me lots of different things to photograph.
